      What you need to know about Company Registration with E&C today

      WHAT IS REQUIRED TO REGISTER A COMPANY?

      • Proposed Company Names (3-5 names in order of preference)
      • Main business Objective
      • Full Names of Directors (at least 2)
      • ID Number of each Director and Address
      • If you are a foreign national a Passport copy is a must
      • Business Address or Home Address
      • Shareholding Structure
      • Email Address of any of the Directors
